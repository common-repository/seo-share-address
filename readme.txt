SEO ShareAddress Plugin for WordPress will auto analyse your post content to exchange inner-POST text links with other WordPress bloggers completely hands free..

== Description ==
You could spend hours upon hours contacting other Blog webmasters to trade links. Then, you would have to spend more time ensuring that the links that you traded are still up. 

SEO ShareAddress will auto analyse your post content and show your each post link in other related Blogs posts.
You will have multiple one way or multi way inner-post links pointed to your Blog.
 


*		Auto Build one way or multi way Inner-POST text Link on other content related blogs.
*		Link with your each POST, Not only one link for your blog.
*   Drive targeted visitors to your own blog by keyword specific.
*   Display up to 6 related post links to other blogs to increase your inner page links.
*   Increase your search engine rankings by automatically building Real Text link.Not show by javascript.
*		Easy installation.Only one step. No configure need. Working with out-of-box.
*   Complete Free.


== Installation ==

Upload the Shareaddress plugin to your blog, Activate it, then enter your [shareaddress.com API key](http://shareaddress.com/).

e.g.

1. Upload `shareaddress.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
